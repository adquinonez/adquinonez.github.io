I am currently a first-year Ph.D. student in the mathematics department at the University of Texas. Born and raised in the Dallas-Fort Worth area, I graduated from North Mesquite High School in 2021 and completed a B.S. in Mathematics and a B.A. in Computer Science at Rice University in 2025.

In my spare time, I enjoy collecting/building LEGO models, practicing the piano, lifting weights, playing video/board/card games, and building PCs.

My current research interests lie in quantum algebra and representation theory, although this is subject to change in the next few years. While at Rice, I conducted research on extended Frobenius algebras under the direction of Dr. Chelsea Walton. I am currently in the process of searching for an advisor at UT so my research interests will likely change over the next few years.
